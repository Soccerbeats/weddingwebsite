Card Hover Effect

When someone mouses over a card on desktop, what should happen?

A

Lift + slight zoom

Card rises up (translateY) and the background image subtly zooms in. Feels premium, modern.

B

Overlay brightens

The dark gradient overlay lightens on hover, revealing more of the photo underneath. Soft and elegant.

C

Gold accent border appears

A gold border fades in around the card on hover. Minimal, ties into the site's accent color.

D

No hover effect

Cards are static. The link behavior (cursor change) is the only indication they're clickable.
